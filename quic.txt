=== macOS ===

Just run Chrome with:

/Applications/Google\ Chrome.app/Contents/MacOS/Google\ Chrome \
    --user-data-dir=/tmp/chrome \
    --no-proxy-server \
    --enable-quic \
    --quic-host-whitelist="serafin.tech" "serafin.tech"

You should then see in the inspector window that connection is established over QUIC:

https://serafin.tech/quic.jpg
